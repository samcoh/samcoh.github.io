Resume

I am a senior at the University of Michigan School of Information with a focus on Information Analytics. My educational journey has developed my passion for investigating datasets, finding valuable patterns within data, asking critical questions, and drawing conclusions by using statistical analysis shown through visualizations. Throughout my college experience, I have made an effort to gain valuable experience writing Python programs, querying SQL databases, and building web applications using Django, HTML, PHP, and JavaScript.

I have been able to improve my technical skills outside the classroom through my experience working as a Data Science intern at Tempus, and as a Senior Editor for the Data Science Engineering team at the Michigan Daily Newspaper. During my time at the Michigan Daily, I deepened my knowledge of Python and Tableau to analyze large datasets and create visualizations of my findings for stories. At Tempus I further developed my expertise in writing Python and SQL when I built an infrastructure that could forecast data needs. My experiences have allowed me to build upon my knowledge of various languages and platforms to construct databases and conduct analytics of complex datasets.

Over the past three semesters, I have worked as a Teaching Assistant for an Introductory to Python class at the University of Michigan. I have built strong communication skills by gaining practice explaining technical concepts to those who are unfamiliar with these topics. My strong communication skills coupled with my ability to handle large and complicated datasets, and communicate complex findings simply, has made me a valuable asset to organizations.
